This guide will explain the features available in the stellarspace app

Planets

The Planets section takes you on a journey through our solar system. Click on each planet to learn about its unique characteristics, atmosphere, and interesting facts.

Quiz

Test your knowledge of the cosmos with the Quiz section. Choose a planet and answer engaging questions to see how much you've learned.

Livestream

Tune in to the Livestream and experience a live broadcast from the International Space Station! Witness the wonders of space exploration firsthand.

Blog

Explore the Blog & Gallery section to delve deeper into space exploration. Read informative articles and view breathtaking images of the universe.
