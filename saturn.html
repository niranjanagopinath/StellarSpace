F A C T S

Saturn is a gas giant like Jupiter. It is made mostly of hydrogen and helium. Saturn has a lovely set of seven main rings with spaces between them.
The Cassini spacecraft orbited Saturn for 13 years, studying Saturn, its rings, and its moons much more thoroughly than the earlier spacecraft could.
Saturn has been known since ancient times. The planet is named for the Roman god of agriculture and wealth, who was also the father of Jupiter.
