Uranus also has faint rings. The inner rings are narrow and dark. The outer rings are brightly colored and easier to see.
Uranus was discovered in 1781 by William Herschel in Great Britain. Uranus has only been visited by Voyager 2.
Uranus's atmosphere contains H2, He, and CH4, and its striking blue-green hue is due to the haze created by methane and hydrogen sulfide gases.
